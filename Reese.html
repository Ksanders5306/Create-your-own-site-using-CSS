Meet Reese

Reese was brought to our shelter from an airport! She is a typical tortie in every way! She is best suited for a family with no kids in the home or other animals. Her favorite time of the day is dinner time and she NEVER misses a meal! She also loves to be petted and will be your shadow everywhere you go.
